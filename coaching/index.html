Personal Coaching

Are you looking to create and sustain long-term changes in your life?

As a coach, my goal is to give you the tools and self-understanding so that you can feel empowered to pay attention to your intuitions and work towards your dreams. I take a holistic view of a person, and identify patterns in what holds someone back as well as strategies to work around or overcome those patterns. Together, we'll work on creatively problem solving and exploring new options that you may not have considered already.

I've worked with people through:

  • Career changes
  • Navigating breakups with long-term partners
  • Working through relationship uncertainties
  • Early stage solo founder & cofounder issues
  • Health goals
